GAMEPAD MAPPING:
-	Home: arm mode
-	Touchpad: drive mode
-	Select: exit mode and reset
Drive Mode:
-	Dpad up/down: move forward and back
-	O: increase speed
-	square: decrease speed
-	Trigger Lower Right: Forward right
-	Trigger Lower Left: Forward left
-	Trigger Upper Right: Reverse right
-	Trigger Upper Left: Reverse left
Arm Mode:
-	Dpad right/left: arm right/left
-	Dpad up: arm forward
-	Dpad down: arm back
-	O: Rotate claw right
-	square: Rotate claw left
-	triangle: elbow up
-	X: elbow down
-	Trigger Right: Tighten Claw
-	Trigger Left: Loosen Claw
